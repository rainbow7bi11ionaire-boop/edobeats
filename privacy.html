Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Edo Beats" application ("we", "our", "the App") handles information when it connects to third-party platform APIs, including the TikTok API, to publish our own original instrumental background music (BGM) videos.

1. Information We Access

When the App is authorized to connect to a TikTok account that we own and operate (e.g. edobeats.music), it accesses, via the TikTok API and only with the account owner's explicit OAuth consent:

  • Basic account information (TikTok open_id and display name) through the user.info.basic scope, to confirm the authorized account.
  • Content publishing access through the content posting scope (e.g. video.publish / video.upload), used solely to upload our own original BGM videos to that same account.
  • An OAuth access/refresh token issued by TikTok to perform the above actions.

The App is used only to post our own content to our own accounts. It does not collect, access, or process the personal data of any other TikTok users, viewers, or third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Accessed information is used exclusively to: (a) authenticate the connected account, and (b) upload and schedule our own music videos. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

3. Storage, Retention & Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally on a private, access-controlled machine and are used only to call the platform API. We do not transmit tokens to any third party. Tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the App and are deleted when access is revoked or no longer required.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any accessed data or tokens with third parties. No data is shared for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Revoking Access & Deletion

The account owner can revoke the App's access at any time from the TikTok account settings ("Manage app permissions"). Upon revocation, the corresponding tokens are invalidated and deleted from our systems. To request deletion of any stored data, contact us at the email below.

6. Third-Party Platforms

Our videos are published on TikTok, YouTube, and other platforms. Each platform collects and processes data under its own privacy policy; please review TikTok's Privacy Policy for details on how TikTok handles data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: edobeats.music@gmail.com
