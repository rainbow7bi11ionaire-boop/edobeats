About

Edo Beats is a small independent music studio producing original Japanese-themed instrumental background music. Our goal is simple: create calming, high-quality BGM that helps people focus, relax, and rest.

All compositions are our own original work. We publish our videos and audio ourselves to our own official channels on YouTube, TikTok, and streaming platforms — we never post on behalf of anyone else.

Is the music free to listen to?
Yes — our music is freely available to stream on our official channels for personal listening.
Do you use vocals?
No. Edo Beats is purely instrumental, designed as unobtrusive background music.
How often do you release?
We publish new tracks on a regular schedule across our channels.
Can I use your music in my own videos?
Please contact us for licensing inquiries before using our content.
